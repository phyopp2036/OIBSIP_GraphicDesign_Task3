Task 3: Business Card Design - Golden Five

Objective
To design a premium, double-sided business card that reflects the luxury theme of the brand.

Tools Used
Canva 

Steps Performed
 
Background Pattern: Used a white marble design with geometric gold mosaic lines to represent high-end tiles.

Central Shape: Added a clean white diamond layout in the center of both sides to give structure to the text.

Front Side Setup: Placed the golden house logo icon, the shop name "GOLDEN FIVE", and the business tagline centered nicely on the front page 

Back Side Setup: Kept the same logo symbol at the top of the back page to stay consistent .

Contact Details: Added the shop's phone number, email address, and store location in a simple black font.

Visual Communication: Paired each contact detail with a matching dark round icon to make the business card clean, modern, and easy to read.
